High-quality teaching materials ensure your child's learning goals are met.

Generally, I focus on the four foundational skills: speaking, listening, reading, and writing. However, the advantage of learning English privately is customization. We will prioritize your child’s improvement areas and turn them into areas of strength.

In Teacher Jonny’s classroom your child will learn to:

Think and Communicate

Your child will learn to think in English and communicate solely in English while in the classroom. Jonny’s classes follow a total English immersion strategy.

Analyze and Interpret

Your child will learn to analyze the information presented in class and interpret the meaning based on their exisiting skills and knowldedge.

Respond and Express

Your child will learn how to respond and express themselves like a Native English speaker by using proper intonation, pronunciation, and facial expressions.
