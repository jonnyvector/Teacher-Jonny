My Mission

Enabling students to fulfill their full potential is what drives me as a teacher. Growth happens when students are pushed to their limit and each class I make sure students are challenged to speak in complete sentences using all of the concepts and vocabulary that we've previously learned.

My Education

I have a bachelors degree in Business Administration from the University of Alabama. I graduated with honors in the 2010 class. I also have a 120-hour in class TEFL certificate. In the past 7 years I have taught myself to speak, read and write the Thai language, so I know first-hand what it takes to learn a new language. This gives me the skills and patience required to be the best possible teacher.
